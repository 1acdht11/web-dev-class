Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookie Baking Tools

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the vanilla and egg. beat on low speed until just incorporated
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
  8. Let cool on wire racks.

Enjoy your cookies!
